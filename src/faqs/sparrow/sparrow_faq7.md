<h4 class="text-2xl pb-4 text-[#f7931a] font-semibold">Recovering or Importing an Existing Wallet</h4>

Wallet recovery can be done using any software that supports **<a class="text-[#8cb4ff] underline-offset-auto font-semibold" href="https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/BIP_0039" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="blank">BIP39<a>** 
mnemonic seeds. If you followed this guide, including Sparrow's quick start guide, than you've 
got a BIP39 mnemonic seed. 

<br>

**1\.** From the top menu or middle of the page click on **File** then click on **Import Wallet**.

<br>

<a href="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_import.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="sparrow wallet recovery import" src="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_import.png" alt="sparrow wallet recovery import" width="800"/> 
</a>

<br>

**2\.** Assuming you're recovering using your 12 word mnemonic seed, select the first dropdown and click on **Use 12 Words**. 

<br>

<a href="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_dropdown.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="sparrow wallet recovery dropdown" src="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_dropdown.png" alt="sparrow wallet recovery dropdown" width="800"/> 
</a>

<br>

**3\.** Enter the 12 words you copied when originally setting up your wallet. They must be in the correct order. 
        Enter a **passphrase** if you used one when originally creating this wallet. Click on **Discover Wallet**. Discovery may take a minute.

<br>

<a href="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_seed.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="sparrow wallet recovery seed" src="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_seed.png" alt="sparrow wallet recovery seed" width="800"/> 
</a>

<br>

**4\.** When asked, add a name to this wallet. You'll be asked to add a password, this is optional (It has no relation to your passphrase).

<br>

<a href="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_name.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="sparrow wallet recovery name" src="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_name.png" alt="sparrow wallet recovery name" width="800"/> 
</a>

<br>

**5\.** If the details you entered are correct, your balance (if you had one), along with all transactions will start appearing. 
 
<br>

<a href="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_compelete.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="sparrow wallet recovery complete" src="./../../../sparrow_wallet_recovery_complete.png" alt="sparrow wallet recovery complete" width="800"/> 
</a>

<br>
