### [How do I receive bitcoin?](#bluewallet-receiving-bitcoin)


<h4>Receiving Your First Transaction:</h4>

**1\.** From the main page tap on your newly created wallet. 

<a href="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_wallet_screen.jpg" target="_blank">
    <img id="bluewallet wallet screen" src="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_wallet_screen.jpg" alt="bluewallet wallet screen" width="400"/> 
</a>

**2\.** On the bottom of the screen tap on **Receive**.

<a href="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_receive.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="bluewallet receive" src="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_receive.png" alt="bluewallet send" width="400"/> 
</a>

**3\.** Confirm that you have written down the 12 words, tap on **YES, I HAVE**. *Make sure you wrote them down!*

<a href="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_backup_confirmation.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="bluewallet backup confirmation" src="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_backup_confirmation.png" alt="bluewallet backup confirmation" width="400"/> 
</a>

**4\.** You'll be presented with a QR code and a public bitcoin address **(starts with bc1)**. 
        The sender can scan the QR code to send bitcoin directly to this address or you can tap on it to copy the address and share it. 

<a href="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_receive_address.png" target="_blank">
    <img id="bluewallet receive address" src="/guide-images/bluewallet/bluewallet_receive_address.png" alt="bluewallet receive address" width="400"/> 
</a>

**5\.** You can also tap **Share** at the bottom of the screen to share the address using a messaging app on your phone.

Once the bitcoin is sent it must be included in a block (confirmed/mined). The transaction will appear as ***Pending*** below ***Transactions***. 
You'll notice it says **0 conf**. It takes a minimum of 1 confirmation before you can consider the bitcoin yours. 
It is typically recommended to wait for 6 confirmations before the transaction is considered settled. 1 confirmation typically takes 10 minutes (on average).
