🔑 Using a passphrase?
A passphrase is an extra secret you choose (any characters, up to 100). It is not one of your recovery words; it is combined with them during key derivation. Do not write it on this sheet. Keep it somewhere separate from your seed, such as a secure password manager or a second piece of paper stored in another location. Back it up too, because losing your passphrase means losing access to your bitcoin.
