<h4>I recovered my wallet but there are no transactions</h4>

There are some common reasons as to why this could happen.

<ul>
    <li><strong>You entered the wrong words.</strong> Any combination of 12 words will create a valid wallet. This is why you must make sure that you entered your seed words exactly as they appeared when first creating it. <em>The same words, in the same order</em>.</li>
    <li><strong>You entered the wrong passphrase.</strong> You entered the correct 12 words but the wrong passphrase, this too will generate a valid wallet, however it won't be the same you originally created. Make sure you enter the correct passphrase (if you used one when creating the wallet).</li>
</ul>

For more details take a look at Sparrow's FAQ: 
<a href="https://sparrowwallet.com/docs/faq.html#all-my-addresses-have-changed" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Wallet Recovery</a>.
